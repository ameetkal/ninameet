About Aix-en-Provence

+

Aix-en-Provence is a charming city in southern France, known as the "City of a Thousand Fountains" and famous for its elegant boulevards, historic architecture, and vibrant café culture. Located in the heart of Provence, it's surrounded by lavender fields, olive groves, and the stunning landscapes that inspired artists like Cézanne.

The city offers a perfect blend of history, art, and French joie de vivre. Stroll down the famous Cours Mirabeau with its plane trees and fountains, explore the medieval old town, or visit one of the many art galleries and museums. The Mediterranean climate ensures sunny days perfect for outdoor dining and leisurely walks.

How to Get There

+
By air:

Recommended if coming directly from the US

The closest airport is Marseille Provence Airport (MRS). It is then a 25 minutes drive to Aix-en-Provence, you can easily get a uber.

By train:

Recommended if coming to Aix after a stay in Paris.

High-speed TGV trains run from Paris Gare de Lyon to Aix-en-Provence TGV station (3 hours). The TGV station is 15 minutes from the city center. You can get there by shuttle or uber.

By bus:

Recommended if coming to Aix after a stay in Marseille

There are buses running every 30mn between Marseille Gare Saint Charles (train station) and Aix-en-Provence city center. The bus number is "Lecar Aix-Marseille L050"

By car:

Not recommended (unless you are going on a drive along the French Riviera!)

You will not need a rental car once you are in Aix as Ubers are easily available

Where to Stay

+

We recommend booking a stay anywhere near downtown Aix-en-Provence. Cheap ubers run throughout the day and night in Aix and downtown is a ~10 minute drive from the wedding venue. We encourage early bookings since Aix is a very popular summer vacation destination.

Things to Do

+

You will have plenty of time to stroll around Aix-en-Provence on Saturday morning and early afternoon before the day's wedding festivities begin. We highly recommend spending time exploring Marseille (30 minutes from Aix) before or after the wedding events. Not only is Marseille one of the most beautiful port cities in the world, it's also where Nina grew up!

Attractions in Aix:

Cours Mirabeau - The city's main boulevard with fountains and cafés
Cathedral of Saint-Sauveur - Beautiful medieval cathedral
Musée Granet - Art museum with works by Cézanne and others
Atelier Cézanne - The artist's former studio
