What is cabaret?

You might be wondering: what exactly is cabaret?

In short: it’s your chance to surprise your friends — and yourself.

Past acts have included:

  • Whispering in the ears of an ancient soul
  • A poem or a song
  • A dance number (with or without the ability to dance)
  • A magic trick
  • A dramatic re-enactment of a film scene
  • An urgent communication from aliens
  • Any form of self-expression you can dream up

Perform solo or as a group — anything goes. To make space for all performers (and still get a night of sleep), we will likely limit each act to just a few minutes.

If you have any questions, reach out to us :)
