Our Story

How we fell in love

March 2023

Farewell Housemates

We first met when we became roommates at Farwell, a co-op style house in Harvard Square. Nina actually interviewed Ameet as a prospective housemate, and one of his first lines to her was "I'm not here to make friends." We all know how that played out.

September 2023

Salsa Dancing

We spied each other on the dance floor at parties and both had an interest in learning how to dance salsa. We decided to become dance partners to learn how to dance, and it didn't take long before the chemistry formed.

October 2023

First Kiss

We resisted the budding romantic feelings for all of one month, although it felt much longer. After a classic MIT 99Fridays night, we kissed for the first time on top of a sculpture.

January 2024

"I Love You"

During our first weekend ski trip together, we shared our love for each other for the first time.

May 2025

Engagement

Ameet surprised Nina at the French National Library to propose before their trip to Japan. With the help of Lucas and Chechi.

June 2025

Calvin Purchase

We purchased our first home together in Somerville, MA with our friends Marcus and Al. Calvin is a 9-person home and our effort to build the chosen family.

June 2026

Wedding

And here we are folks - we can't wait to celebrate with you and begin the next adventure with you.
